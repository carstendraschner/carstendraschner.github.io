## Background

After an incredible time at **Comma Soft AG**, it’s time to close this chapter with deep gratitude.  
The experience combined early-stage engineering, applied AI/LLM research at **Alan.de**, and machine learning projects for external enterprises — plus the joy of contributing to PR and thought leadership.

## What have I done:

I worked on developing and applying AI systems that connect research with enterprise applications, helping bridge the gap between foundation model innovation and real-world impact.  
The work environment was both intellectually stimulating and personally enriching, thanks to a team of kind, talented, and inspiring colleagues.

## IMHO:

This journey has been one of collaboration, learning, and growth.  
It showed me the beauty of building intelligent systems together with passionate people who genuinely care about their craft and about each other.  
To my teammates, and especially to Michael Tannenbaum, Lars Flöer, Sebastian Schönnenbeck, Andrej Fischer, Benjamin Schulte, Lukas Pfahler, PhD, and Dr. Laura Maaßen (l2r on the picture) thank you!
