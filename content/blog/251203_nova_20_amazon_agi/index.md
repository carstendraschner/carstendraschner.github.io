## Nova 2.0 - Amazon AGI Foundation Models Released

Its already an amazing time at Amazon AGI with great colleagues and here are officially the next generation of Nova Models presented at this years Amazon Web Services (AWS) Re:invent developed by the Amazon AGI in-house foundation model R&D efforts.

It is a family of Nova models:

- **Lite:** Cost Performance efficient, like Haiku/Flash/Mini
- **Pro:** Top Notch Model
- **Sonic:** Speech to Speech
- **Omni:** Multimodality io
- **Forge:** Trainable Models
- **Act:** Agents

Models are already in use at companies like Reddit, Inc., Sony, Cisco, Siemens, Sumo Logic, Trellis, Booking.com, Cosine AI, Nimbus Therapeutics, NRISG Nomura Research Institute, Principle, Amazon Leo, Hertz, 1Password (see Blogpost)

You can try Nova Lite for Free through Open Router (see References). If you are interested in the existing Usecases or the in-house Trainium Silicon Checkout the Video. More details available in Amazon Science or full video on YouTube

Looking already ahead to improve our next Gen of AI!
