## Background

That was fun to speak at **Comma Talk 25**! ❤️  
AI is happening in the heart of the EU industry, and events like this show how diverse and impactful the applications are across Europe and Germany.  

## What have I done:

I had the pleasure of delivering a keynote with Andrej Fischer where we demystified the hype around (Gen)AI and highlighted valuable trends.  
Later, I co-hosted a **GenAI-Roundtable** with Florian Götz, where we had exciting and unfiltered discussions about real-world use cases.  

It was inspiring to see so many great talks from industries such as:  
- 💻 Industry SWE (Siemens Healthineers: Agnes Loehner)  
- 🏗️ Production (Kautex Textron: Bernhardt Lüddecke)  
- 🚗 Automotive (Daimler Truck AG: John Dalton)  
- 🛡️ Insurance (VHV Gruppe, Generali, SDK)  
- 🏦 Banking (Nassauische Sparkasse)  
...and more, including a fantastic session on demystifying the **EU AI Act** by Paula Cipierre. 📜🤖  

## IMHO:

Events like Comma Talk are crucial for pushing European and German AI forward. They bring together experts, industry leaders, and researchers to exchange valuable ideas and highlight impactful real-world AI/ML use cases.  

Huge thanks to **Comma Soft AG** and the co-hosts for organizing this inspiring event.  
And yes – the Jazzfest evening was the perfect way to wrap it up. 🎷🎶
